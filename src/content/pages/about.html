About Us

Waffle House Bar & Grill

Welcome to Waffle House Bar & Grill, located inside Super 8 by Wyndham Sherwood Park. Since opening in November 2023, we have been dedicated to providing a dining experience that combines unique flavors with a welcoming atmosphere. Our menu features a delightful fusion of Western and Asian cuisine, going beyond just waffles and desserts. We serve breakfast, lunch, and dinner until 2 AM, ensuring you can enjoy our mouthwatering dishes at any time of day.

Our space includes a pool table, a lively bar, and VLT machines for your entertainment. With a passion for diverse flavors and a rich background in the food industry since 2010, our owner brings a special touch to every dish. Whether you're here for our signature waffles and steaks or simply to enjoy a vibrant and friendly environment, Waffle House Bar & Grill offers something truly exceptional.
